IronLog is a minimalist, native Android application for logging your strength training sessions. 

Features:
- Fast logging with shorthand input (e.g., 135x5x3)
- Automatic Personal Record (PR) tracking
- Estimated 1RM calculations
- Exercise history and progress tracking
- 100% private: all data stays on your device
- Open source and free from tracking or ads

Inspired by the simplicity of a paper log but enhanced with the power of native Android.
